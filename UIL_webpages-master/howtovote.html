How to vote for transit condition in your neighborhood

  1. Home
  2. How to Vote

    Have a different opinion about the transit condition in your neighborhood? You can share your opinions with us directly!

    Step One:
    Click on one block group that you’re familiar with:

    Step Two:
    Give us your opinion about whether this area is a transit desert by choosing from the two options in the pop up info window.

    Step Three:

    Show us the reason by clicking on the checkbox, it would be great if you provide other useful information by briefly describing the transit conditions around the neighborhood.

    Step Four:
    Click “submit” button, you will be redirect to a page indicating that we have successfully received your opinion. The page will automatically close in a few seconds, continue voting!
